Asmile is afacial expression formed primarily by flexing the muscles at the sides of the mouth.It is believed it takes about 80 muscles to smile. Somesmiles include acontraction ofthe muscles at the comerof the eyes, an action knownas a "Duchennesmile". Smiles performedwithout the eye contraction maybe perceived as insincere.
